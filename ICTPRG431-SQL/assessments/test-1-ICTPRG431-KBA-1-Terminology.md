### Question 8
Complete  

*Research and explain in your own words what a composite key is. Give a real world example of something you could store with a composite key.*

**Selected Answer:**  
A composite key is a key derived from two or more fields in order to make it unique. in real life, this might be a shipping ID derived from the date and time when the shipment was organised as well as a client code or ID.
