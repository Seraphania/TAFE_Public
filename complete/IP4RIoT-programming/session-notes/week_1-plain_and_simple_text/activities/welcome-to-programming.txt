Why Plain Text is Awesome
•	Simplicity
o	Easy to read and write with no special software required.
•	Versatility
o	Compatible across different platforms and environments.
•	Control
o	Offers complete control over the content without hidden formatting.
Benefits of Using Plain Text
1.	Universality: Can be opened and edited with any text editor.
2.	Lightweight: Consumes minimal disk space.
3.	Durability: Less prone to corruption compared to proprietary formats.
Embracing plain text as a beginner in programming offers a strong foundation for learning and growth.
