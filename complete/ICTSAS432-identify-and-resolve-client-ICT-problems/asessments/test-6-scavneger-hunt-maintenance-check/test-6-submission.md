### Question 1
Unmarked 	

**View [this website0](https://www.hp.com/us-en/shop/tech-takes/10-essential-computer-maintenance-tips) to answer the below question.**  
Explain why organizing cords is important.

Selected Answer: 	
```
Managing External cables is important, cables lying where they might be tripped over/kicked/or knocked obviously poses a hazard to anybody that might trip, it alos poses the additional danger that repeated wear might cause the internal wires to become exposed - for example if cables are rubbed between a wall and a table. This poses and electrocution hazard. it also has the potential to damage the equipment the cable is attached to - for example if a laptop cable is pulled on the laptop may fall off a table.

Internal cable management withing hardware is also important, having cables well organised makes it easier to trace where they are going during maintainence. It can also improve airflow around components, and reduce the risk of cables getting stuick in fans or otherwise causing problems by kncking into hardware.
```

### Question 2
Unmarked 	

**View [this website](https://www.minitool.com/backup-tips/computer-maintenance.html) to explain the difference between preventative and corrective mainentice,**  

Selected Answer: 	
```
Prevenative maineainence aims to correct issues before they become problems, this includes software maintainence such as keeping antivirus up to date, freeing disk space, and freeing up ram by removing unnecessary utilities.

Physical preventative maintenance will include things like cleaning fans, and ensuring components are properly seated.

Corrective maintenance is performed after a problem has occured. Examples include removing malware (software) or replacing a faulty component (hardware).
```

Explain hardware maintenance in your own words.


Selected Answer: 	
```
Hardware maintainance means taking care of the physical componenets of a computer, including peripherals. Example hardware maintenace tasks may include:

* Dusting fans and internal compoonenets
* Cleaning keyboards
* Cleaning monitor screens
* Cable management
* cleaning the heads of tape drives
* checking cables for signs of wear.
```
